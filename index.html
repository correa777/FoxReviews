The Invisible Guest

Year of Release: 2016

Director: Oriol Paulo

Summary: "The Invisible Guest" is a Spanish movie full of mystery and suspense. It's about a man named Adrián Doria (played by Mario Casas) who wakes up in a hotel room next to a woman who is dead. He didn't do it, but he needs to prove his innocence. So, he hires a lawyer named Virginia Goodman (played by Ana Wagener) to help him. As the story unfolds, many secrets come out, and there are lots of surprises along the way.

Review: "The Invisible Guest" is a movie that will keep you guessing and make you think. The director, Oriol Paulo, does a great job telling the story. The actors, especially Mario Casas and Ana Wagener, are really good. They make you feel like you're trying to solve the mystery right alongside them. The movie has many surprises and plot twists. Sometimes, you might find the story a bit complicated, but that's okay. It's still a very interesting movie. It makes you question what's real and what's not, and that's part of the fun. I would recommend "The Invisible Guest" to people who like mysteries and movies that keep you engaged. It's a movie that will hold your attention from the beginning to the end. Even if you're not from Spain, you can still enjoy it because it's a good mystery. Give it a try, and you'll have a fun time trying to figure out the story and its surprises.

Rating: 4.6/5

Miracle in Cell No.7

Year of Release: 2019

Director: Mehmet Ada Öztekin

Summary: "Miracle in Cell No. 7" is a very touching movie from Turkey. It's about a man named Memo (played by Aras Bulut İynemli) and his daughter, Ova (played by Nisa Sofiya Aksongur). Memo has some problems in his head, but he loves Ova a lot. But one day something bad happens, and Memo goes to prison, even though he didn't do anything wrong. While in prison, he makes friends with other prisoners, and they try to help him see Ova again. It's a very emotional and heartwarming story.

Review: "Miracle in Cell No. 7" is a movie that will make you feel a lot of emotions. The actors, especially Aras Bulut İynemli, do a great job, and you can really see how much Memo loves Ova. The story is funny and sad at the same time, and you'll like the friends Memo makes in prison. This movie shows that love between a parent and a child is very powerful. Even if the movie is from Turkey, it doesn't matter where you're from. "Miracle in Cell No. 7" is a story that will touch your heart and make you think about how important love is. The way it's filmed and told is really good.

Recommendation: We think everyone should watch "Miracle in Cell No. 7," especially if you like movies that make you feel emotional and sensitive. It's a very special story about love and how people can help each other. Even if you don't know much about Turkish movies, this one is worth watching. Get ready to cry and smile, because this movie will make you do both.

Rating: 4.7/5

Life Is Beautiful

Year of Release: 1997

Director: Roberto Benigni

Synopsis: "Life Is Beautiful" is a heartwarming Italian movie that happens during World War II. It's about Guido, a dad, and his son Giosuè, who end up in a tough place, like a special camp, but in reality it’s a Nazi concentration camp. To make things easier for Giosuè, Guido plays a game with him, pretending it's all a big adventure, like a challenge to win a tank. This movie tells a powerful story about people staying strong, love, and finding happiness even in very hard times. Review: "Life Is Beautiful" is an amazing movie that mixes funny and sad moments to tell a really special story. Roberto Benigni, who plays Guido and directed the movie, does an incredible job. He shows Guido as a dad who makes his son feel safe and happy, even when things are really bad. It's a movie that will make you laugh and cry. This movie is great at showing how people can keep going even when things are tough. It also shows how strong love is and how important it is to protect the people we care about. "Life Is Beautiful" is a movie that leaves a huge impression. It reminds us that even when life is hard, there's still a lot of good in the world. It's a movie that will touch your heart and make you feel emotive.

Recommendation: We really suggest watching "Life Is Beautiful" if you like good stories, great acting, and movies that make you feel heartwarming and sensitive at the same time. This movie is like a piece of art that will stay with you for a long time. It shows how people can be strong and find happiness, even when life is really tough. "Life Is Beautiful" is a special movie that everyone should see at least once.
